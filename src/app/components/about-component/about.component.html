About 
  
The family beeing splitted across the world, it is not easy to keep in touch

    with relatives. Of course we usually know our close family, some of our direct cousins,

    but that's about it. When it comes to second cousins, we marely know how we are related.

  
  
Purpose

  
The purpose of this website is to learn about our genealogy. The aim is to collaborate,

    to build the family tree so it can be usefull for the next generation.

  
  
Solution

  
I tried some tools available on the market to build family trees, but didn't find anything

    that fits what I was expecting. Something easy to use and accessible to everyone.

  
  
Features

  
- Family Tree and user information

    - Photos

    

    In the future, it would be interesting to have some features like messaging,

    contact infos, birthday reminders, GEDCOM generation ...

Data privacy

  
For the moment, all information are public. Except dates, places and contact infos, that are

    only availble to connected users.

    All information you consider sensitive, should not be added. Please also avoid put photos

    unless authorized by concerned persons.
